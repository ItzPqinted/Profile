Ghost Tower Runner V1 BETA

Is it Halloween yet? This game suits it sort of. A Ghost that goes up a tower, avoiding wooden-stand.
*This is pretty incomplete; It has some bugs.

Specs :

-You need a Desktop/Laptop to play.
-Chrome Browser/Microsoft Edge (This game will lag just a bit (15 FPS) on Mozilla Firefox Browser)
-This can run good on weak PCs.
-CPU Usage upto 30%

-Game Resolution : 600x600 (Browser)

Instructions :
Press the SPACEBAR Key to hover/fly. Use the LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys to move. You have to keep tapping the SPACEBAR Key to hover and don't hit the stand(the wood from below) instead let the ghost stand on the wood so that you can get a break.
Don't go out of the Canvas! The Objects (Wood, Windows) come from the top of the screen. No score or feedbacks exist in the game.
