My Quiz / Riddle V1 BETA (Mobile Browser Support)

Quiz time? Damn, so early?
For this game, you basically need 4 players in order to play. Don't have any friends? No problem, after clicking the below link and opening the tab, duplicate the tab so that you can have all-together 4 tabs. Now, you have 4 'friends'!

For instructions, you can scroll down in that tab to get the steps. Can't understand? Let me share some.

This is a riddle basically. Read the question and the options. Then, type in your name in the input 'Name'. Make sure you DIRECTLY type in your name! Don't try to press the BACKSPACE key! Why? Just don't.
Afterwards, type in the OPTION NUMBER and don't press the BACKSPACE key, or else you won't be able to find the correct answer.
After doing everything, click on SUBMIT. This information will go to My Database. After all the 4 players type in their information correctly, this database will check all the answers.
After checking, the database will mark the opponent and answer "GREEN" for the one who has answered it correct, and mark RED for the one who hasn't answered correctly.
And, that's it! Job's done! Also, I can read the data obviously, so type in your name in a decent way!
